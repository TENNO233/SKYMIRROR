OneMap - Singapore's National Map Platform

OneMap is the authoritative and most comprehensive map of Singapore,
developed and maintained by the Singapore Land Authority (SLA). As
the nation's official geospatial platform, OneMap provides the most
detailed and up-to-date mapping information available.

Designed to serve as a trusted source of geospatial data, OneMap
enables users to access a wide range of street-level information and
perform location-based queries. From land ownership details to
nearby amenities such as schools, parks, and healthcare facilities,
OneMap offers granular insights to enhance decision-making,
planning, and everyday navigation. Whether for daily use, research,
education, or planning, OneMap is a trusted and accessible source of
accurate, localised geospatial information.

OneMap API

OneMap offers range of APIs designed to serve the diverse
mapping and geospatial needs of government agencies,
businesses, associations, third-party developers, researchers,
and the general public.
