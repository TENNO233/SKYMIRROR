Instructions to Applicants

(A) Documents to be Submitted

1. To apply to undergo trial of Automated Vehicle Technology or Autonomous Motor
Vehicle (AV), please complete and submit the following:

a) Form AV01: Application for Authorisation to Undertake Trial of Automated
Vehicle Technology or Autonomous Motor Vehicle;

b) Form AV02: Details of Each Vehicle (one form to be submitted for every
vehicle); and

c) Form AV03: Details of Each Vehicle Model (one form to be submitted for every
vehicle model).

2. Each AV intended to be used in the Trial must have an on-board AV Operator, to progress
to driverless deployment of AV, please complete and submit Form AV04: Application
for Driverless Deployment.

3. All documents submitted MUST be in the English language. For documents that are not
presented in English, notarised translations1 are acceptable.

4. LTA will notify successful applicants in writing and may require the AVs and the
automated technology to be subject to safety assessments and inspection before the AVs
are authorised to be trialled.

(B) Safety Assessments and Inspection

5. Each AV intended to be used in the Trial must first clear a vehicle safety assessment at
the CETRAN AV Test Centre and inspection at any location identified by LTA, before
the Trial may be commenced.

6. Prior to trialling in autonomous mode on any public road, please ensure that:

a) each AV has a mechanism to engage and disengage the autonomous technology
that is easily accessible to the operator;

b) each AV has a visual indicator inside the cabin to indicate when the autonomous
technology is engaged;

c) each AV has a system to safely alert the operator of the AV through visual and
audio alerts to take control of the vehicle if a technology failure is detected,
including loss of communication;

d) each AV allows the operator to take control in multiple manners, including,

(C) Authorisation and Chargeable Sums

7. For the purpose of the Trial, the relevant authorisation under the Road Traffic
(Autonomous Motor Vehicles) Rules 2017 (“Authorisation”) must be granted before the
Trial can commence. Terms and conditions may be imposed on the conduct of the Trial,
and LTA may impose terms and conditions in respect of each AV to be used in the Trial.
Unless contrary to the context, a ny reference to “Trial” in this document (including its
enclosures) is a reference to the Trial subject to the Authorisation and such terms and
conditions imposed by LTA.

8. The sum2 payable for the use of each AV authorised for use in the Trial (“authorised
AV”) is:

a) $1,600 for 12 months

b) $800 for 6 months

c) $250 for 1 month

d) $30 for 1 day

9. In addition, an administrative fee of $27 is payable for each AV.

10. A vehicle number will be assigned in respect of each authorised AV to be used for the
Trial. Applicants must fabricate the vehicle number plates for each authorised AV
according to specifications at Annex A: Specifications for Vehicle Number Plate. The
vehicle number plates must be sealed at any LTA -authorised inspection centres and the
seal shall not be broken without prior approval from LTA.
